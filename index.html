Creative Director

Beli yang Perlu, Elak Barang Palsu

  • Agency: ReAliss
  • Role: Creative Director
  • Project Type: Integrated Marketing Communication Campaign
  • Year: 2026

As Creative Director of ReAliss, I led the creative vision for our Integrated Marketing Communication campaign titled "Beli yang Perlu, Elak Barang Palsu". The campaign aimed to educate consumers about counterfeit products while encouraging responsible purchasing behaviour. My responsibilities included developing the campaign concept, directing the visual identity, ensuring consistency across all communication materials, and collaborating with designers, copywriters, and media planners throughout the creative process. Campaign deliverables included billboards, posters, social media content, and presentation materials that collectively communicated a cohesive and impactful campaign message.
